Veratus / About

A small group with a long horizon.

Veratus Group is an independent technology company. It's a solo studio — one founder shipping software and games people want to use every day.

01 Who we are

We build things people keep open.

Veratus Group brings two disciplines under one roof: productivity software for the Mac, and original mobile games. They look different on the surface, but they come from the same place — a belief that the best products are fast, opinionated, and finished.

Veratus is a solo studio. One founder ships every product, top to bottom — design, code, release, support. That keeps scope tight, decisions fast, and accountability obvious. The group structure exists so each brand can stand on its own as the work scales.

02 How we work
